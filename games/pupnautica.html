DEEP SEA EXPLORER

PERSONAL LOG - PLANET 4546B

The Aurora starship suffered a catastrophic failure. I barely made it to Drop Pod 7 before the explosion. The pod crashed into this endless alien ocean. The life support systems are heavily damaged, and rescue is not coming anytime soon.

I am stranded.

Thankfully, the pod's Hydraulic Printer and Storage Locker survived the impact. The printer is like a highly advanced crafting table—it can rearrange atomic structures to build survival gear, provided I have the raw materials.

My PDA sensors detect abundant resources in the Limestone Outcrops scattered across the seabed. Breaking them should yield Copper, Titanium, or even the peculiar alien flora the PDA calls "Sea Rice".

I need to dive, gather materials, and craft better gear if I want to live another day. But I must watch my oxygen.

Controls: WASD to move, Mouse to look, Space to ascend, Shift to descend. Left Click to interact.
